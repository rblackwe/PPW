The [% site.event_name %] will be held at the DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel & Suites in downtown Pittsburgh.

The DoubleTree Hotel is located about 18 miles from Pittsburgh International Airport. Taxi faire is about $45. You can also take the Super Shuttle for about $24.

Limited parking is available at the hotel (valet only) for $22. There are additional parking garages with self-parking located nearby. (We suggest the US Steel Building as a near-by alternative.) [Pittsburgh Parking Options]

New for 2015: The hotel will be providing a $5 parking discount for anybody attending PPW. Also, anybody that has a room in the hotel will be able to keep their car in the garage on Sunday until the end of the event.

Where to Eat

A continental breakfast, snacks and beverages will be provided on the day of the workshop. We will also provide vouchers ($10/day) that may be used in the hotel restaurant for lunch or an evening drink. We hope to obtain sponsorship so that we may also include our social event on Saturday night for free.

A 7-Eleven convenience store is located on the exterior of the hotel building. (Exit through the main lobby entrance and turn right). A number of fast food and restaurant options are available within walking distance of the hotel. Additionally, there is a hotel shuttle that will take hotel guests to most locations in the local Pittsburgh area.
